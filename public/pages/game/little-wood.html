The Hero Who Saved the World

Explore the vast world of Solemn. Enchanted Forests, Bustling Fishing Towns, & Dark Mining Caves are some of the few places to visit. Meet Townsfolk and convince them to stay in your town. Perhaps meeting people will unlock your memories of the past… Collect rare items and materials found across the world and bring them back to your town for selling and crafting!

Customize Your Town

Harvest materials by chopping wood, mining ores, catching bugs, and fishing in rivers and ponds! Unlock new structures to build by meeting new Townsfolk. Want a Tavern or a Lumber Mill? How about a Fishing Hut or a Magical Library? Your town's design is all up to you! Fulfill your Townsfolk's requests. Some may want to live on a higher elevation, others may want to be surrounded by trees and water. Fulfilling the requests of your Townsfolk will earn you their hearts and unlock new relationship paths.

养花秘籍： 1、首先你要有浇花的工具，站在花上交互，过几天花边上就会出现新花，野外的花只能采不能浇，花只能在家里培育。
2、有六种花型，最基础的三种在野外能找到，其余三种要用基础花两两杂交，就是种在一起每天浇水。
3、每种花八种颜色，野外能找到的红白黄，树林里遇到的NPC边上有蓝色，或者几朵白色一起浇水会变异出蓝色，然后把四种颜色种在一起，每天浇水，就会出现粉色、紫色、橙色，把几朵紫色拎出来种在一起，就会出现最后的特殊颜色。
4、只有一朵花每天浇水也能增殖，但会很慢，几朵一模一样的花种在一起能加快增殖，一两朵紫色花在一起能长出紫色，但紫花一多周围就只会张特殊颜色的花了。
